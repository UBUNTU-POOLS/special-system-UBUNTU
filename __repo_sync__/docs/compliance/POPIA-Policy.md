
# POPIA Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction
Ubuntu Pools (Pty) Ltd ("we", "us", or "the Platform") is committed to protecting the personal information of our kinsmen in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

## 2. Information We Collect
For the purpose of community trust and FICA compliance, we process:
- **Identifying Data**: Full name, SA ID/Passport Number, Biometric Liveness Data.
- **Contact Data**: Email, Phone (ShapID).
- **Social Capital Data**: Contribution history, vouching records, and governance participation (The Ubuntu Score).

## 3. Purpose of Processing
Data is processed strictly for:
- Maintaining the non-custodial community ledger.
- Generating the trust reputation (Ubuntu Score).
- Compliance with the Financial Intelligence Centre Act (FICA).

## 4. Cryptographic Redaction
Upon member request for data erasure, Ubuntu Pools implements a "Cryptographic Redaction" protocol. Chained event hashes remain to preserve audit trail integrity, but PII metadata is permanently purged using industry-standard erasure techniques.

## 5. Information Officer
All POPI requests should be directed to the Platform Information Officer at compliance@ubuntupools.co.za.
